DWS | GOLDEN HONEYCOMB (GHC) PAGE SPECIFICATION

1. Purpose
The GHC page introduces the Golden Honeycomb as DQ’s core operating system for modern work. It explains why traditional work models fail, what the Golden Honeycomb is, and how it is applied in practice across strategy, delivery, capability, and leadership at DQ. This page is conceptual and explanatory, not a service or marketplace page.

2. Target Users
- New joiners learning DQ’s operating model
- Existing associates seeking clarity on how DQ works
- Leaders and practitioners applying GHC concepts

3. Page Structure & Sections

3.1 Hero Section
- Purpose: Frame the problem with modern work and position GHC as the response.
- Content: Headline highlighting the broken nature of traditional work; short supporting statement introducing GHC; primary CTA button.
- Functionality: CTA navigates to the GHC Storybook; no user input or operational actions.

3.2 What Is the Golden Honeycomb?
- Purpose: Explain GHC’s operating DNA, its adaptability, and its seven elements.
- Content: Section title; one-line explanation positioning GHC as an operating system; three concept cards — Operating DNA; Built for Change; Seven Elements; CTA to explore the full GHC Storybook.
- Functionality: Cards are informational; CTA links to detailed GHC documentation.

3.3 Seven Responses in Action
- Purpose: Show how GHC addresses failures in traditional work models.
- Content: Section title; short description; category tabs representing GHC perspectives — Vision; House of Values; Persona; Agile TMS; Agile SoS; Agile Flows; Agile 6xD; visual cards for each response with short descriptions; CTA to explore all seven responses.
- Functionality: Tabs filter content by perspective; cards link to Knowledge Center or related GHC content.

3.4 Bring It to Life
- Purpose: Demonstrate how GHC moves from concept to practice.
- Content: Section title; one-line supporting description; four action cards — Storybooks; Learning Center; Knowledge Center; Viva Engage.
- Functionality: Cards link to learning, knowledge, and community platforms; encourages adoption and deeper understanding.

3.5 Work Aligned Inside the Golden Honeycomb
- Purpose: Connect GHC to daily work inside the Digital Workspace.
- Content: Section title; short explanation of how GHC aligns tools, decisions, and work; primary CTA button.
- Functionality: CTA directs users to the Digital Workspace; serves as the bridge between GHC and execution.

3.6 Footer
- Purpose: Provide navigation and reference information.
- Content: DQ Digital Workspace branding; navigation links (Learning, Knowledge, Services, Communities); legal, support, and version information.
