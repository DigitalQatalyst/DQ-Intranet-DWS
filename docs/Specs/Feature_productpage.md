DWS | DQ PRODUCT PAGE SPECIFICATION

1. Purpose
This page helps new joiners understand:
- What DQ Products are
- How they connect to Agile 6xD and the 6x Digital Framework
- How products are structured
- Where products apply across sectors
- How associates engage with them

2. Target Users
- New joiners during onboarding
- Associates moving into delivery roles
- Internal teams needing clarity on DQ product structure

3. Page Structure

3.1 Hero Section
- Purpose: Introduce the idea that transformation fails when execution is fragmented.
- Onboarding role: Explain why DQ builds structured products instead of ad-hoc solutions.
- Content: Headline about fragmented execution; short explanation; two CTAs — Explore Storybook and Explore Product Marketplaces.
- Functionality: CTAs route to internal learning or marketplace sections.

3.2 Built on the 6x Digital Framework
- Purpose: Show that products are structured, not random.
- Content: Six framework cards — Digital Economy; DCO; Digital Business Platforms; Digital Transformation 2.0; Digital Worker & Workspace; Digital Accelerators.
- Onboarding role: Connect products back to the framework already introduced in Agile 6xD.

3.3 The Four Classes of DQ Offerings
- Purpose: Explain internal product classification.
- Content: Four classes — DBP Services; DT 2.0 Product; DCO Product; TxM Platform.
- Card requirements: Name; short explanation; internal action (Explore / Learn More).

3.4 Driving Transformation Across Sectors
- Purpose: Show where products are applied.
- Content: Sector groupings — Cross-Sector Initiatives; Primary Sector; Secondary Sector; Tertiary Sector; Quaternary Sector.

3.5 Choose Your Path
- Purpose: Help associates decide where to go next.
- Content: Three cards — Start with DTMA; Explore DQ DTMI; Explore Product Marketplaces.
- Onboarding role: Help associates understand where their work fits and what products are.

3.6 Execution Becomes System. System Becomes Scale.
- Purpose: Close the page and direct users to next steps; reinforce that DQ turns execution into structured systems and scalable products.
- Content: Headline “Execution Becomes System. System Becomes Scale.”; supporting line “Every DQ product begins in live delivery and is built to scale.”
- Buttons: Explore DQ Products; Explore DQ Services.
- Navigation: Explore DQ Products → DQ Website Products page; Explore DQ Services → DQ Website Services page.
