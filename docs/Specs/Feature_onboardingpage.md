DQ | ONBOARDING PAGE SPECIFICATION

1. Purpose
The DQ Onboarding Page is the structured entry point for new and existing associates to understand how DQ operates. It provides a guided journey across four foundational pillars:
- DQ Organization
- Golden Honeycomb of Competencies (GHC)
- DQ 6xD Digital Execution Model
- Your Role as a Qatalyst

The page is designed to reduce onboarding confusion and provide clarity on structure, expectations, and execution standards.

2. Objectives
- Provide a clear 3-month onboarding pathway
- Introduce DQ’s organizational model and internal structure
- Explain competency expectations through GHC
- Clarify how execution happens through 6xD
- Reinforce ownership and impact mindset
- Provide accessible support channels

3. Target Users
- New joiners within their first 90 days
- Existing associates needing structural reset
- Internal mobility associates transitioning roles
- Leads onboarding new team members

4. Page Structure

4.1 Hero Section
- Headline: Welcome to DQ Onboarding
- Supporting statement explaining the 3-month onboarding journey
- Primary CTA button: View the 3-Month Onboarding Guide
- Intent: Clarity and direction with a single primary action to reduce cognitive overload

4.2 Section 01 – DQ Organization
- Purpose: Introduce internal structure and operating model
- Content must explain: DQ is built for execution (not hierarchy), clarity of units and delivery flows, internal navigation understanding
- CTA status: Coming Soon (future behavior links to detailed DQ Organization page)

4.3 Section 02 – Golden Honeycomb of Competencies (GHC)
- Purpose: Define behavioral and delivery expectations
- Content must communicate: GHC governs how associates think, collaborate, and deliver; seven structured competencies; performance alignment reference
- Primary CTA: Explore GHC → navigates to dedicated GHC landing page

4.4 Section 03 – DQ 6xD Digital Model
- Purpose: Explain how work execution happens
- Content must clarify: 6xD is DQ’s operational execution engine; it connects planning, validation, and delivery; it ensures ideas convert into measurable outcomes
- Primary CTA: Explore Agile → navigates to detailed 6xD explanation page

4.5 Section 04 – You Are the Qatalyst
- Purpose: Reinforce ownership and individual accountability
- Content must communicate: every associate is a value driver; impact is measured through collaboration and execution; ownership mindset is expected
- CTA status: Coming Soon (future behavior links to personal development or growth pathway page)

4.6 Support Section
- Headline: Need Help Along the Way?
- Purpose: Provide structured support access
- Support cards (all required): People Partner; DWS Communication Center; FAQs & Glossary
- Each card must include: short description; clear “Get in Touch” CTA; internal routing link
